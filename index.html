Frequently Asked Questions

What about International Tours?

Yes we do have International Tours! We have Tours to Sri Lanka, Thailand, India, Saudi Arabia and More! 

What do Qatar boat tours offer?

Our Qatar boat tours offer stunning views of the Doha skyline and the Arabian Gulf. Enjoy a relaxing cruise with options for fishing and water sports.

What about buggy and quad bikes?

Experience the thrill of the desert with our buggy and quad bike adventures. Our expert guides ensure a safe and exhilarating ride through the dunes.

What is included in safaris?

Our desert safaris include camel rides, dune bashing, and traditional Bedouin hospitality. Enjoy a magical evening under the stars with delicious local cuisine.

What will I see on tours?

Our historical tours take you through Qatar's rich heritage, visiting landmarks like Al Zubarah Fort and the National Museum. Learn about the country's fascinating past and vibrant culture.

What about Eurofox plane rides?

Take to the skies with our Eurofox plane rides and experience breathtaking aerial views of Qatar. Our certified pilots guarantee a safe and unforgettable adventure.
